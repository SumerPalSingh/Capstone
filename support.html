Support

Welcome to the support page of ISL Academy. We're here to help you with any questions or issues you might have while using our platform to learn and practice Indian Sign Language. Below you will find various resources and ways to get in touch with our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the sign recognition work?

Our platform uses advanced AI technology to recognize your hand signs through your device's camera. Simply follow the on-screen instructions, and the AI will guide you through learning and practicing Indian Sign Language.

What do I need to use the platform?

You will need a device with a camera and an internet connection. Our platform works best on a modern web browser such as Google Chrome or Firefox.

Can I track my learning progress?

Yes! Our platform allows you to track your learning progress, revisit lessons, and take quizzes to reinforce your knowledge.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing technical difficulties, please try the following steps:

  • Ensure your camera is enabled and functioning correctly.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Try accessing the platform from a different browser or device.
  • Check your internet connection.

If the issue persists, please contact our support team for further assistance.
